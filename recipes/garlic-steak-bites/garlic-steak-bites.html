Garlic Butter Steak Bite

This recipe is for busy weeknights when you don't have time for something fancy but want to have a tasty quick meal. The beef can be done in 15-20 minutes can be paired with white rice and steam borrocolis.
It's very quick, you while you put on the rice the beef will be done too.
Before we get to the ingredidents let me share with you some very useful tips ahead.

INGREDIENTS

For the moist cake

  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 500 g ground beef
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/2 tps fresh ginger, minced
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper
  • 1/2 cup sliced green onions
  • salt pepper to taste

DIRECTIONS

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium heat.
  2. Brown the ground beef in the sesame oil.
  3. Add the garlic towards the end of browning and cook for a couple of minutes.
  4. Drain the fat.
  5. Add the brown sugar, soy sauce, ginger, salt and pepper and red pepper.
  6. Simmer for a few minutes to blend the flavors.

It is all done. Enjoy your flavourful meat with with rice and steamed broccolis!

Carrot Cake

This recipe is one of my favorites. When I first tried this carrot cake I fell in love with the flavour and moistness.
It's very easy to make. You don't need to be a skilled baker to get this one right.
Before we get to the ingredidents let me share with you some very useful tips ahead.

INGREDIENTS

For the moist cake

  • 260 g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tps salt
  • 1 1/2 tps ground cinnamon
  • 300 ml vegetable oil
  • 200 g granulated sugar
  • 190 g brown sugar
  • 1 tps vanilla extract
  • large eggs, at room temperature
  • 300 g carrots, peeled and grated (5-6 medium sized carrots)
  • 100 g coarsely chopped pecans

For the creamy frosting

  • 225 g cream cheese, (room temperature)
  • 140 g powdered sugar
  • 80 ml heavy whipping cream
  • 50 g pecans, coarsly chopped for topping cake
  • 300 ml vegetable oil

DIRECTIONS

For the cake

  1. Heat the oven to 180 Celsius.
  2. Whisk the flour, baking soda, salt, cinnamon) until very well blended.
  3. Mix in another bowl the oil, granulated sugar, brown sugar and vanilla.
  4. Whisk in the eggs one at a time.
  5. Whisk in the dry ingredients to the other bowl in three parts.
  6. Divide the batter in two cake pans.
  7. Bake for 35-40 minutes and check with toothpick test.
  8. Let it sit for 15 minutes then carefully take out form the pan. Let it cool down completely.

For the frosting

  1. Beat the cream cheese in a large bowl until creamy.
  2. Mix in the powdered sugar in four parts.
  3. Pour in the whipping cream and beat for 2 to 3 minutes untill thefrosting resembles the texture of whipped cream.
  4. Put the frosting on the cake, to the top, sides and between the two halves.
  5. Whisk in the dry ingredients to the other bowl in three parts.
  6. Finish with a handful of pecans on top.

It is all done. Enjoy your flavourful dessert!

Carrot Cake

This recipe is one of my favorites. When I first tried this carrot cake I fell in love with the flavour and moistness. It's very easy to make. You don't need to be a skilled baker to get this one right. Before we get to the ingredidents let me share with you some very useful tips ahead.

INGREDIENTS

For the moist cake

  • 260 g all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tps salt
  • 1 1/2 tps ground cinnamon
  • 300 ml vegetable oil
  • 200 g granulated sugar
  • 190 g brown sugar
  • 1 tps vanilla extract
  • large eggs, at room temperature
  • 300 g carrots, peeled and grated (5-6 medium sized carrots)
  • 100 g coarsely chopped pecans

For the creamy frosting

  • 225 gc ream cheese, (room temperature)
  • 140 g powdered sugar
  • 80 ml heavy whipping cream
  • 50 g pecans, coarsly chopped for topping cake
  • 300 ml vegetable oil
